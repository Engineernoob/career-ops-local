# Mode: interview

**Triggered by:** `ops interview <report-id>`

## What This Mode Does

Builds a targeted interview prep kit for a specific role.
Pulls relevant STAR stories from interview-prep/story-bank.md.
Generates new stories if gaps exist. Predicts questions.

## Output Sections

### 1. Role Analysis
What competencies is this company likely testing?
(Technical depth, leadership, ambiguity, execution, culture)

### 2. Predicted Questions
10 questions, labeled by type:
- Behavioral (STAR format expected)
- Technical/systems
- Situational
- Company/mission fit
- "Why us / why this role"

For each question:
- The question itself
- Why they're asking it
- Which STAR story from story-bank.md to use (or draft one if missing)
- One-line tip

### 3. STAR Story Mapping
For behavioral questions, map to existing stories in story-bank.md.
Flag any competency with no story → draft a new one and add to story-bank.md.

### 4. Questions to Ask Them
5 strong questions the candidate should ask:
- One about the team's current biggest challenge
- One about what success looks like in 90 days
- One about the technical roadmap
- One about the interviewer's experience
- One that shows research (company-specific)

### 5. Your Opening Pitch
2-minute "tell me about yourself" tailored to this role.
Hits: current role, key achievement, why this company specifically.

## STAR Format

**S** — Situation: Context (brief, 1–2 sentences)
**T** — Task: What you were responsible for
**A** — Action: What YOU specifically did (not "we") — most important part
**R** — Result: Quantified outcome, business impact

Keep each story under 2 minutes when spoken aloud.
